Reynolds Number:


The Reynolds number is calculated using the following formula:

Re = ρ V L / μ

Where:

  • ρ is the density of the fluid (kg/m³)
  • V is the velocity of the fluid (m/s)
  • L is a characteristic length of the flow (m)
  • μ is the dynamic viscosity of the fluid (Pa⋅s)

The Reynolds number is a dimensionless number that is used to characterize the flow of a fluid. It can be used to predict the type of flow (laminar or turbulent), the frictional forces, and the heat transfer.
